# Business Case

## Background
Olist is one of Brazil's largest e-commerce marketplaces.

The management team wants to better understand sales performance,
customer behaviour, delivery efficiency and customer satisfaction.

## Objectives

1. Analyse sales trends

2. Identify high-value customers

3. Evaluate product performance

4. Assess delivery efficiency

5. Improve customer satisfaction

## Expected Outcome

Provide recommendations supported by SQL analysis.
