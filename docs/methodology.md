# Methodology

This project follows a workflow to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights.

## Step 1: Database Understanding
- Review the Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD).
- Understand the relationships between customers, orders, products, sellers, payments, and reviews.
- Identify primary and foreign keys for table joins.

## Step 2: Data Quality Assessment
- Check for missing values.
- Identify duplicate records.
- Examine data types and potential inconsistencies.
- Validate key relationships between tables.

## Step 3: Exploratory Data Analysis
- Calculate high-level business metrics.
- Understand the scale of the business.
- Explore order status, customer distribution, seller distribution, and product categories.

## Step 4: Sales Performance Analysis
- Analyze sales trends over time.
- Identify top-performing states, cities, products, and categories.
- Evaluate revenue patterns and seasonality.

## Step 5: Customer Behavior Analysis
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Identify repeat customers.
- Calculate average order value.
- Evaluate customer lifetime value (where applicable).

## Step 6: Delivery Performance Analysis
- Measure delivery lead times.
- Identify regions with slower deliveries.
- Evaluate order fulfillment performance.

## Step 7: Customer Satisfaction Analysis
- Analyze review scores.
- Investigate the relationship between delivery performance and customer satisfaction.
- Identify product categories with consistently high or low ratings.

## Step 8: Business Recommendations
- Summarize key findings.
- Provide data-driven recommendations.
- Discuss limitations and opportunities for future analysis.
